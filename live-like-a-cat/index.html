Live Like a Cat

When someone asked me as a kid what I would want to be when I grow up I always picked a cat, I can see why the ancient Egyptians revered them as sacred animals. Pet cats get waited on hand and foot by their envious owners, they live a stress free, enjoyable life. I keep seeing new incredible advances in Science, Technology, Robotics, AI and Automation and this website explains how they will allow society to have all their necessities taken care and allow more time spent on hobbies and passions and allow me to fulfil my life long dream of living like a cat.

We can see new technology which can produce abundant renewable energy and soon harness nuclear fusion to create an endless supply of cheap Energy. Once we have abundant energy it can be put to use turning sea water in fresh Water, using this to grow abundant amounts of food in new advanced higher yielding indoor farms, soon everyone will be vegetarians eating meat grown in labs.

The Materials we consume can be created using 3D printing. Health and Education looks to get much better thanks to technology, even the poorest will soon have access to the world best teachers and doctors.


Computers are getting exponentially more powerful, driving the capabilities of robot and automation tools, if they replace workers at a faster rate than job creation can keep up with then solutions to allow everyone to House themselves and still have an Income are discussed.
This website looks at the trends that are likely to happen in the near future and how we can use them to allow us more Leisure.
